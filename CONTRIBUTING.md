# Contributing to Research

Thank you for contributing to the research workspace around the Panta Rhei framework.

## What belongs here

Use this repository for:

- paper source materials
- exploratory research notes
- notebooks and scripts
- topic-specific research folders
- issue threads around research questions
- pull requests that improve or extend research-facing materials

## What does not belong here

Please do **not** treat this repo as:

- the official public PDF/DOI surface (`papers`)
- the canonical Atlas content source (`atlas`)
- the formal implementation workspace (`taulib`)

## Recommended contribution pattern

1. open a discussion or issue for substantial new lines of work
2. keep PRs scoped and well explained
3. explain the relation to books, parts, topics, or results where possible
4. prefer explicit structure over large unstructured drops of material

## Folder growth

New folders and new topic areas are welcome when they are:

- clearly named
- explained in a short README
- connected to a real research line
- not duplicating existing structure without reason

## Conduct

Please follow the organization-wide code of conduct.
